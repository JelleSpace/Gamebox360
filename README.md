# Gamebox360
Gamebox 360 - Open Source  Mobile Game Launcher Gamebox 360 turns your Xbox 360 ROMs and mobile games into a sleek, searchable library. Metadata magic + one-click launches for Xenia, emulators, and more.
Gamebox 360 – Open Source Mobile Game Launcher
Gamebox 360 is an open-source mobile app designed to transform your Xbox 360 ROMs and mobile games into an organized, easy-to-navigate library. It provides a smooth, user-friendly interface with powerful metadata integration, offering a sleek experience for game enthusiasts. The app allows you to launch your games with just one click, making the experience more streamlined.
Key Features:
Game Library: Automatically organizes your Xbox 360 ROMs and mobile games into a searchable, easy-to-browse catalog.
Metadata Magic: Retrieves game metadata (titles, descriptions, art) for a polished presentation.
One-click Launch: Instantly launches compatible games, with support for Xbox 360 (via Xenia) and other emulators, allowing seamless gameplay on Android devices.
Add-Ons: Offers additional customization options, enhancing the gaming experience.
Compatibility: Works on Android devices running Android 8.0 and above. It's optimized for devices with 4GB RAM, ensuring smooth performance.
Supported Platforms:
Xbox 360 ROMs: Play Xbox 360 games with Xenia and other compatible emulators.
PS2 Games: Supports PS2 games, giving you more options for classic gaming.
Emulators: Runs various emulators for a wide range of gaming experiences on your Android device.
System Requirements:
Android 8.0 or higher (works well with Android 9.0, 11, and 13).
Adreno 618 GPU (optimal performance with this GPU model).
Supports ROMs up to 6-8GB in size for compatibility.
With Gamebox 360, you can enjoy your favorite Xbox 360 and PS2 games, all from the convenience of your Android phone, without needing to deal with multiple apps or complicated setups. It's a one-stop launcher for gamers who want to streamline their retro gaming experience.
